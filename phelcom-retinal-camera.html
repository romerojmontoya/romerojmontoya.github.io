Overview

This guide walks IT support staff through provisioning a new store account and linking a Phelcom retinal camera to the EyerCloud platform. Once linked, retinal images captured at the store will automatically sync to the EyerCloud web portal for review and recordkeeping.

Who performs this task

IT Support — Tier I / II

When to use this guide

New store · Camera replacement · Re-linking disconnected device

Devices affected

Phelcom Eyer Retinal Camera

Outcome

Camera linked to EyerCloud and retinal images syncing to the store account

Prerequisites

Have the following before performing installation:

RoboForm access (IT Department folder)
Store number is known
Phelcom camera is powered on and connected to Wi-Fi
Access to the store's email inbox
ℹ

All credentials are managed through RoboForm

The Phelcom Retinal Admin credentials are stored in the IT Department folder in RoboForm and should always be accessed from there. Do not log in manually.

1

Access the admin site

1

Open RoboForm and navigate to the IT Department folder.

Locate the entry labeled Phelcom Retinal Admin and use it to log in to [ADMIN PORTAL URL].

2

Confirm you are logged in to the correct organization.

After logging in, the portal header should display [Organization] [Store#]. If a different organization name appears, log out and repeat step 1.

2

1

Navigate to Configurations.

Click the gear icon at the bottom left of the portal to open the Configurations panel.

2

Open the Users tab and click Invite.

Under the Users tab, scroll to the bottom of the user list and click Invite. Enter the store email address using the format [store#]@[domain].com and set the permission level to Technician.

3

Accept the invitation using the store's email account.

Use RoboForm to access the store's email inbox at [store#]@[domain].com. Open the invitation email from EyerCloud and click the activation link. Complete account setup using the credential table above.

4

Save the new login to RoboForm.

After the account is created, add the store's EyerCloud credentials to the store's RoboForm folder so they can log in independently going forward.

3

Link the camera

1

Have the store representative log in to EyerCloud.

Guide the store rep to open a browser and navigate to [ADMIN PORTAL URL]. Have them log in using the RoboForm entry saved in their store folder.

2

Navigate to Configurations and open the Devices tab.

Once logged in, click the gear icon to access Configurations, then select the Devices tab.

3

Click Add New Device to generate a QR code.

A QR code will appear on screen. Keep this browser window open and visible — you will need it in the next steps.

4

On the camera, open the EyerCloud app and access clinic settings.

Power on the Phelcom camera and open the EyerCloud app. Locate the Clinic Name field and tap the double-arrow icon beside it to open the clinic selection screen.

5

Select "Access with QR Code" and scan.

On the clinic selection screen, tap Access with QR Code. Point the camera at the QR code displayed on the browser to complete the pairing. The camera will confirm the connection once the scan is successful.

⚠

QR codes expire

The QR code generated on the Devices tab is time-limited. If the scan fails or takes too long, return to the Devices tab and click Add New Device to generate a fresh code.

4

Verify setup

1

Confirm the device appears under the Devices tab.

Refresh the EyerCloud Devices tab in the browser. The newly paired camera should appear in the device list with an active status.

2

Capture a test image and verify it syncs.

Have the store rep capture a test image using the camera. Return to the EyerCloud portal and confirm the image appears in the store's image library. This confirms end-to-end connectivity.

✓

Setup complete

If the device appears in the portal and the test image synced successfully, the camera is fully configured. Inform the store that the Phelcom camera is ready for patient imaging sessions.

Troubleshooting

QR code scan fails or is not recognized

Ensure the browser displaying the QR code is not zoomed in or cropped. If the code has been on screen for more than a few minutes, it may have expired — return to the Devices tab and generate a new one. Confirm the camera has an active Wi-Fi connection before trying again.

Invitation email was not received

Check the store's spam or junk folder. If the email is not present after a few minutes, return to the Users tab in EyerCloud and verify the email address was entered correctly. You can re-send the invitation from the same screen.

Test image does not appear in the portal

Confirm the camera completed the QR scan and shows the correct clinic name on the EyerCloud app home screen. If the clinic name is missing or incorrect, repeat the device linking steps. Also verify the store has a stable internet connection.

Unable to log in to the admin site

Always use RoboForm — do not attempt to log in manually. If RoboForm auto-fill fails, check that you are using the Phelcom Retinal Admin entry under the IT Department folder. If the issue persists, the credentials may have been rotated — contact the IT helpdesk.
